**ssemQr** is an R package for joint eQTL mapping and gene regulatory network inference.

**Key features:**
- Improves detection of both cis- and trans-eQTLs
- Gene regulatory network inference under genetic perturbations
- Structural equation modeling framework

Published in *Bioinformatics*, btab609, 2021.
